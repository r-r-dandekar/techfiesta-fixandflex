Ask A Question

What types of loans do you recommend?

We analyze home, car, personal, and education loans, including alternatives like home top-ups and refinancing.

How accurate are the approval chances shown?

Approval probability is calculated using credit score, income stability, DTI ratio, and lender rules—making it highly realistic, not a guess.

Is my financial data safe and private?

Yes. Your data is encrypted, never shared without consent, and used only for personalized recommendations.

Can I compare multiple loan options side by side?

Yes. You can compare loans on EMI, total interest, approval probability, and flexibility—all in one view.

What happens after I take a loan?

We continue to support you with proactive alerts for refinancing, prepayment savings, and better rate opportunities.
